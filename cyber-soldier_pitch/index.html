Cyber soldier — tactical online/offline shooter

Cyber soldier — is bounty hunter action shooter with realtime multiplayer and offline modes.
Key features:
+ Original, unique gameplay
+ Co-op
+ Characters with distinctive skills
+ Lots of weapons
+ Different locations for matches
+ Nice, stylized graphics
/ The game has a pretty unique gameplay. The look of the game is similar to Dead Ahead, but with free character control. The game modes also have their own features, "Deathmatch" and "Wingman", where the main goal is to earn more currency, for which you can purchase unique weapons during the game, and which is credited to your account after the match. Inside the match, when a character dies, a certain amount of money is taken away, which is credited with a bonus to the killer. This is a kind of Bounty Hunter mode. There is also Matchmaking and Skill Groups, with a flexible selection of players. It works roughly like in CS:GO. For currency, you can unlock new characters and weapons, upgrade characters. There is also a small quest system and an additional reward for consecutive victories. The target audience is from ~10 to 22 years old. Now the game has already been published on Google Play — https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.WnatureGames.Achernar It is almost ready for publication in the App Store, it has already been tested on a real device. The project has been alive for quite a long time, during this time we managed to fix a lot of bugs in the game and add improvements. There have been 170 downloads in the last 30 days. The promotion of the game was not used, but somehow we were able to find players. This is far from the best period in the life of the project, there was a time when downloads were gaining momentum and reached 2,893 downloads in a month, but then we are changed the name from Achernar to Cyber soldier and the metric abruptly hit zero the very next day. That was over a year ago. It didn't work out much from the project yet, but it wasn't supposed to, because the game is only now good enough and thought out.
